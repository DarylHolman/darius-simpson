About Darius

Darius Simpson is an African American writer, performing artist, and activist born in Akron,Ohio. He is recognized globally for his award-winning spoken word poetry. He received his Bachelor Degree in Political Science from Eastern Michigan University. He is a community organizer featured in Forbes Magazine for his volunteerism. Darius uses performance poetry to challenge audiences in their awareness of social, political, and economic oppression. Having performed at major conferences such as TEDxDetroit, his work has also been featured in online publications such as Huffingtonpost, Mic, and Worldstar Hip Hop.
